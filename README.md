# 🌱 About

Future Guardian is an AI-powered sustainability platform designed to encourage environmentally responsible living through intelligent insights, personalized recommendations, and engaging challenges.

The platform transforms everyday eco-friendly actions into rewarding experiences by combining artificial intelligence, gamification, and community participation to promote long-term sustainable habits.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Help users monitor and reduce their carbon footprint
- Promote sustainable daily habits
- Encourage responsible consumption
- Increase environmental awareness
- Inspire youth-led climate action
- Build eco-conscious communities through collaboration

---

# ✨ Features

## 🤖 AI-Powered Sustainability Assistant

Receive personalized eco-friendly recommendations based on your lifestyle and daily activities.

---

## 🌐 Multilingual Support

Future Guardian is designed to be accessible to a wider audience by supporting multiple languages.

Supported Languages:

-  English
-  Tamil (தமிழ்)
-  Hindi (हिन्दी)

Users can seamlessly switch between languages, making AI-powered sustainability guidance more inclusive and easier to understand.

---

## 🌍 Carbon Footprint Tracking

Track your environmental impact through an interactive dashboard that helps visualize daily carbon emissions.

- 🏆 Achievement Badges
